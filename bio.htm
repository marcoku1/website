SHORT BIOGRAPHICAL DATA:


Maria U. Ku, a.k.a. Maria Ugrioumova, was born in 1971 in Moscow, Russia. Her father is a mathematician and her mother was an aircraft engineer in the former Soviet Union. Maria received her early education in a French school with emphasis on Mathematics & Science. She graduated from school in 1988 with Gold Medal of Excellence from the USSR Ministry of Education.

Maria studied in the School of Mathematics and Mechanics at Moscow University until she left Russia for political reasons.

Maria came to the United States in January 1992. She was granted political asylum in the U.S.A. in 1993, and she became a naturalized U.S. citizen in 1999.

Maria graduated, with High Honors, from Haas School of Business, UC Berkeley, in 1997.

She has taught accounting classes at the Ohlone College during 2007-2014, Laney College 2015-2016, and the Diablo Valley College since 2009.

Maria has worked in accounting since 1993, including tax practices of the Big 4 public accounting firms. She started her own tax practice in 2000.

Maria is married and has two children, a UC Berkeley and Berkeley Law graduate, and a 5th-year Cal Poly Architecture student, both originally alumnae of Oakland Tech, an Oakland Public School.
